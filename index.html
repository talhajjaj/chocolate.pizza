For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir in dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat ,stirring frequently, until you have a chunky - jammy mixture. And salt with one or two set inuide and let cool completely.

Ice cream: in a small pot over medium heat, combine milk and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm .Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mtixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer Instructions,i.e, let spin and thicken for 20 minutes hofare adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glasperware Freezer for at least two hours before serving.
